Gratitude's Resonance - Reflecting on IMSF



In my time with the IMSF
I've learned a lot about myself
Admittedly, I didn’t apply to the program seeking social change or leadership; I simply sought to continue the work I started in Professor Faraj and Anand’s class.

Though my intentions may have been misguided, they were sincere

Eight months later

I still don’t know that I want to be a leader or a rockstar, but

I feel the potential

To lead myself

To chart my own course
It's not about

making the perfect decision

It's not about

having the perfect internship

It's about pursing more ideas
like data transparency
forging ahead just to try

to see what's possible

It’s about knowing

I have the power to decide what comes next
